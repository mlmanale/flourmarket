FlourMarket's Story

Located and founded in the heart of downtown Los Angeles, FlourMarket's owner, Madison Manale, always had a love for baking. At age 22, she decided to make her passion into her career. Growing up, Madsion's father always had a garden with plenty of herbs, veggies, and flowers, and she always sought to bring the beauty of nature into her homecooked meals.

In the summer of 2022, after years of working out of her own kitchen, Madison purchased a property in downtwon Los Angeles to make her dreams of opening FlourMarket a reality.
